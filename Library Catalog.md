### **Creating Tables** 
**CREATE TABLE** Books ( 
    book_id **INT* **PRIMARY KEY**,    
    title **VARCHAR*(200),
    publication_year **INT*
);

**CREATE TABLE** Author (
    author_id **INT* **PRIMARY KEY**,    
    author_name **VARCHAR*(100)
);
